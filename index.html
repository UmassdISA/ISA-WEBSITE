The Indian Student Association at UMass Dartmouth welcomes you to its home page. Here you will find information about the organization, the Indian community living in and around the campus, and some tips for the new students. We are a non-profit student organization recognized by the University. Our purpose is to provide an opportunity for Indian students to interact and share the joy of our culture and heritage. ISA serves to promote the awareness of cultural diversity on campus by organizing cultural and social events on campus.

We try to ensure a homey atmosphere for Indian students living away from home and unite Indians and people of other races by celebrating and learning from diversity. Our strength as an organization comes from the unconditional commitment of our members and the community.
